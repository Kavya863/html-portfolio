The Best Places Which I Visited in Gokarna

My favorite places in Gokarna

Mahabaleshwara Temple



The temple is one of the seven sacred Muktikshetras or Muktistala ("places of salvation") in Karnataka.


Om Beach



It is located in North Canara district. One of the acclaimed beaches in the country, Om Beach takes the shape of an 'Om', a Hindu spiritual symbol.

Kudle Beach



Kudle Beach is most famous for offering a spectacular sunset and sunrise in Gokarna.
